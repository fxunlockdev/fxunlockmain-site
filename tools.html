Tool 2 - Risk and Lot Size Calculator

Calculate Your Risk in Seconds. Every Trade. No Spreadsheets.

Position sizing is where most traders go wrong. They either skip the calculation entirely, or they use a clunky spreadsheet that breaks their pre-trade routine. Our Risk and Lot Size Calculator is simple, fast, and accurate, built to be used in the moment, right before a live trade. Enter your account size, your risk percentage, your stop loss distance in pips, and the instrument, and get your exact lot size instantly.

  • Instant lot size output based on account size, risk percentage, and stop loss distance
  • All major FX pairs, indices, commodities and metals
  • Clean, minimal interface designed for use before a live trade
  • Fully mobile-optimised

Why it matters for IBs: When your community members manage risk properly, they trade longer, blow fewer accounts, and generate more consistent volume on your rebate structure. This tool directly supports the longevity of your client book.
